Meeting Organiser

Meeting Organiser aka MeetOrg is a simple chrome extension which is developed to schedule Google Meetings

It Automatically Opens the scheduled Google Meet at the correct time and auto attend meet if no user intraction is deteceted

This Extension was my personal tool which I used to automate my Google meeting.
